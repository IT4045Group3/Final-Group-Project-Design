Help

Welcome to Culinary Companion! If you have any questions or need assistance, please refer to the information below:

1. How to Use the Search Feature?

Use the search bar at the top of the page to find recipes. Enter keywords and press the search button to get relevant results.

2. Exploring Categories

Discover a variety of cuisines by clicking on the "Category" section. Each category contains a selection of recipes.

3. Viewing Recipes

Click on the "Dishes" section to see a list of available recipes. Select a recipe to view its details and cooking instructions.
