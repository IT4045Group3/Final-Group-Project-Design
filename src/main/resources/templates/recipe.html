Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 250g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© onions and garlic until translucent.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Break it up with a spoon.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes and oregano. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Serve over cooked spaghetti and enjoy!

Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large pan over high heat.
  2. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  3. Add garlic and stir for 1 minute.
  4. Add bell pepper and broccoli, stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Pour in soy sauce and cook for another 2 minutes.
  6. Serve hot over rice or noodles.
